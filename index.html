Hi! We are Hacksmiths, the award winning student run tech society at Goldsmiths, University of London.

Our mission is to provide everyone, regardless of their circumstance or experience, a change to learn about, play with, and explore technology.

If you have questions, please feel free to get in touch at team@goldsmiths.tech
